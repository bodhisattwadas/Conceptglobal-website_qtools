Research and Industrial Clients

Our Customers

We work closely with leading research organizations and technology companies to deliver precision scientific and industrial solutions.

At Quantum Tools and Services, we work closely with leading research organizations and technology companies to deliver specialized research equipment, structural solutions, and custom-engineered systems.

We work with research institutes like the Indian Association for the Cultivation of Science, Kolkata, and the Variable Energy Cyclotron Centre, Kolkata on specialized research equipment and structural solutions. Our expertise in precision-engineered T-slot aluminum structures, vacuum systems, optical assemblies, and custom-machined components allows us to design and deliver systems that integrate seamlessly into advanced laboratory setups.

In collaboration with Metrecon Industries, QTS also undertakes fabrication of customised instruments and process flow development, ensuring end-to-end solutions tailored to specific industrial requirements.
