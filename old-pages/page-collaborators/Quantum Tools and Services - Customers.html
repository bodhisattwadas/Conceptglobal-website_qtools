Our Collaborators

We also collaborate with DEPEQ Technologies to provide microscopy solutions to academia and industry, extending their marketing network while integrating our other products into their systems. This collaboration combines our modular engineering capabilities with DEPEQ’s specialized microscopy expertise to offer complete, ready-to-use solutions for imaging and analysis.

In addition, we are the exclusive distributor for India for DACTools and Razorbill Instruments, supplying advanced high-pressure cells, uniaxial strain cells, and cryogenic-compatible instrumentation to research institutions across the country. Through these partnerships, we ensure that Indian researchers have direct access to cutting-edge experimental tools without compromise on service, support, or customization.

Quantum Tools and Services (QTS) has partnered with TraceXero as a product consultant and fabrication partner for direct carbon capture systems. QTS will provide design optimization, cleanroom process integration, and precision fabrication support for core capture modules. The collaboration leverages TraceXero’s proprietary carbon capture chemistry with QTS’s expertise in micro/nanofabrication and advanced materials handling. QTS will also oversee prototyping, scalability assessments, and custom tool development to streamline system production. This partnership ensures a robust pathway from laboratory concept to industrial-scale direct carbon capture deployment.
