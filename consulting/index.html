Experimental Systems

Consulting Services for Experimental System Design & Integration

Support for developing, optimizing, and integrating specialized experimental setups in condensed matter physics, quantum materials research, and nanotechnology.

At Quantum Tools and Services, we provide advanced consulting services to support the development, optimization, and integration of specialized experimental setups. Our focus lies in enabling workflows that combine fabrication, high-pressure research, strain application, and spectroscopic or electrical measurements customized to scientific objectives.

Areas of expertise

  • Cleanroom Process and Device Fabrication: Guidance on optical and e-beam lithography, etching, deposition, encapsulation, substrate choice, resist chemistry, and process flow optimization.
  • High-Pressure, Low-Temperature, and Magnetic Field Integration: System-level consulting for DACs with cryostats and magnets, including vibration isolation, pressure calibration, optical access, feedthroughs, and stability.
  • Strain-Based Experimentation: Integration of piezoelectric uniaxial strain cells with cryogenic platforms, plus strain calibration, sample mounting, and signal routing.
  • Spectroscopy and Transport Measurement Compatibility: Raman, IR, and THz compatibility checks under pressure or strain, with optical alignment, window design, and shielding support.

Collaborative system development

  • Co-development of custom hardware that integrates equipment across vendors.
  • Retrofitting and adapting tools for specific experimental conditions.
  • Communication support across stakeholders to ensure compatibility and reliability.

Training and technical support

  • High-pressure experimental protocols.
  • Low-temperature and high-field measurement systems.
  • Lithographic device preparation and strain-based transport studies.

This ensures researchers can build, operate, and troubleshoot advanced setups with confidence.
