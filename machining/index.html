Specialty Metals and Scientific Hardware

Precision CNC Machining of Specialty Metals

Advanced CNC machining services for high-performance scientific and industrial applications with tight tolerances and research-grade material handling.

Quantum Tools and Services provides advanced CNC machining services for high-performance scientific and industrial applications. We specialize in the precision fabrication of components using stainless steel, oxygen-free high-conductivity copper, beryllium copper, and tungsten carbide. All parts are manufactured to exacting standards, with tolerances maintained strictly according to design specifications.

Materials and applications

  • Stainless Steel SS304, SS316L, SS430: Corrosion resistance, thermal stability, and compatibility with high and ultra-high vacuum environments.
  • OFHC Copper: High thermal and electrical conductivity for cryogenic applications, thermal interfaces, and RF components.
  • Beryllium Copper C17200, C17510: High strength, fatigue resistance, and electrical conductivity for spring-loaded contacts and non-magnetic hardware.
  • Tungsten Carbide WC, Grade 2: Wear-resistant tooling, indenter tips, and custom parts for high-pressure research.

Machining capabilities

  • Precision CNC milling and turning for complex geometries.
  • Machining optimized for vacuum and cryogenic compatibility.
  • Surface finishing, threading, and fitment for scientific instruments.
  • Quality inspection and metrology for design fidelity and surface integrity.

All components are fabricated using certified, high-purity materials, with full traceability and strict quality control. We support both prototyping and volume production, along with custom machining services for specialized research needs.
