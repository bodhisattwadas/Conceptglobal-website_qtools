Heavy-Duty Aluminium T-Slot Extrusion Profile

High-strength T-slot aluminium framing systems for industrial racks, stacking systems, equipment frames, precision lab structures, and reconfigurable machine builds.

  • Our high-strength T-slot aluminium framing system is built for industrial racks, stacking systems, equipment frames, and precision lab structures.
  • Made from 6061-T6 or 6082-T6 aluminium for superior strength and load-bearing capacity.
  • Delivers corrosion resistance, modular flexibility, and easy reconfiguration without welding.

Features

  • High Strength: High-strength aluminium, up to 370 MPa tensile strength, for heavy-duty loads.
  • Modular Design: Quickly assemble, expand, or reconfigure without welding.
  • Corrosion Resistant: Clear anodized surface for long-term durability.
  • Customizable: Available in multiple profile sizes from 20-60 series and custom lengths.
  • Accessories Ready: Works with T-slot connectors, brackets, gussets, and custom fittings.

Applications

  • Laboratory racks and shelving systems.
  • Semiconductor and cleanroom equipment stands.
  • CNC and automation machine frames.
  • Precision instrument stacks and mounts.
  • Storage and handling trolleys.
  • Optical table framing and enclosure supports.

Technical specifications

  • Alloy Options: 6061-T6, 6082-T6.
  • Surface Finish: Clear anodized, hard anodized, or powder coated.
  • Slot Sizes: 6 mm, 8 mm, 10 mm.
  • Cross-Section Sizes: 20 x 20 mm to 60 x 60 mm heavy-duty variants.
  • Load Capacity: Up to 2,500 kg per structure, design-dependent.
  • Connection Method: T-nuts, corner brackets, gussets, and custom plates.
  • Standard Lengths: 3 m, 5 m, with custom cuts available.
